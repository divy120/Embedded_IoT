About the Drone Club:
The Drone Club at Akgec is more than just a club, it's a community of innovation, technology, and adventure. Our mission is to explore, educate, and elevate the world of drone technology. We envision a future where drones play a pivotal role in various industries, and we're here to shape that future.

Our Goals:

Promote Education: We aim to educate students about drone technology, its applications, and the responsible use of drones.

Foster Innovation: We encourage innovative thinking and provide a platform for members to create cutting-edge drone projects.

Build a Network: We believe in the power of connections, both within the club and with industry professionals.

Safety First: We prioritize safety and responsible drone operation in all our activities.
